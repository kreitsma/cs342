1. a. A flight describes the general information regarding a particular flight that occurs on certain days of the week. A 
	Flight Leg is a segment of a flight; if it is a direct flight, then there is only one leg, but a flight may have layovers. A
	Leg Instance is a particular instance of a Flight Leg which holds data for that specific instance, like the date and 
	number of available seats.
